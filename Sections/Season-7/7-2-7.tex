\SSpsetQ{
    The numbers of ordered touples $(x_1,...,x_{100})$ which satisfy:
    \begin{enumerate}
        \item $x_1,...,x_{100}\in\{1,2,..,2017\}$;
        \item $2017|x_1+...+x_{100}$;
        \item $2017|x_1^2+...+x_{100}^2$
    \end{enumerate}
    can be expressed as a product of its prime factors, \(p_1^{\alpha}p_2^{\beta}\ldots\), find \(p_1\alpha+p_2\beta+\cdots\)
}\bigskip
